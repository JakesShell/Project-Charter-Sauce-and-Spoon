# Project Charter: Implementation of Tabletop Menu Tablets at Sauce and Spoon

<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>
This repository encapsulates the project charter for the strategic implementation of tabletop menu tablets at Sauce and Spoon North and Sauce and Spoon Downtown. This initiative aims to enhance the dining experience by leveraging innovative technology to streamline the ordering process, improve operational efficiency, and ultimately drive revenue growth.
</p>
<h2>Project Summary</h2>
<p>
The pilot rollout of tabletop menu tablets is designed to revolutionize client engagement and optimize service delivery. By integrating cutting-edge technology, this project seeks to achieve a significant reduction in table turn time, minimize food waste, and enhance customer satisfaction. Key performance objectives include increasing daily visitor counts, improving profit margins, expanding the product mix, and boosting appetizer salesâ€”all pivotal for the sustained growth of Sauce and Spoon.
</p>
<h2>Goals and Objectives</h2>
<p>
<ol>
  <li>Reduce Table Turn Time: Aim for a 30-minute decrease in table turn time by the end of Q2 through streamlined ordering and payment processes.</li>
<li>Minimize Food Waste: Achieve a 100% reduction in food waste from erroneous orders, enhancing operational efficiency.</li>
<li>Enhance Customer Satisfaction: Target a 90% improvement in customer satisfaction levels regarding the ordering process.</li>
<li>Increase Daily Visitor Counts: Strive for a 15% increase in daily guests by optimizing customer engagement strategies.</li>
<li>Improve Profit Margins: Seek a 10% enhancement in profit margins through operational optimization and upselling initiatives.</li>
<li>Expand Product Mix: Introduce 10 new menu items to cater to diverse customer preferences.</li>
<li>Increase Average Check Amounts: Raise the average check from $65 to $75 by promoting higher-priced items.</li>
<li>Enhance Kitchen Capacity: Expand kitchen stove capacity by adding two units to accommodate increased order volumes.</li>
<li>Boost Appetizer Orders: Aim for a 15% increase in appetizer sales at Sauce and Spoon North and a 20% increase at Sauce and Spoon Downtown.</li>
</ol>
</p>
<h2>Deliverables</h2>
<p>
  <ul>
<li>Tablet Rollout Pilot: Execution of the rollout within designated bar areas, ensuring effective integration and operation.</li>
<li>Staff Training Program: Comprehensive training initiatives to familiarize staff with the tablet system.</li>
<li>Integration with Existing Systems: Seamless connectivity with current POS systems to ensure operational continuity.</li>
<li>Documentation and Reporting: Development of a robust documentation framework to monitor project progress and outcomes.</li>
  </ul>
</p>
<h2>Scope and Exclusions</h2>
<h3>In Scope:</h3>
<p>
<ul>
<li>Implementation of tablets in designated areas.</li>
<li>Selection and configuration of tablet packages.</li>
<li>Training program development for staff.</li>
<li>Integration with existing restaurant systems.</li>
</ul>
</p>
<h3>Out of Scope:</h3>
<p>
  <ul>
<li>Expansion beyond the designated areas.</li>
<li>Redesign of physical restaurant layouts.</li>
<li>Customization beyond necessary integrations.</li>
  </ul>
</p>
<h3>Benefits and Costs</h3>
<p>
<h4>Benefits:</h4>
  <ul>
<li>Enhanced customer experience through interactive ordering.</li>
<li>Increased operational efficiency and reduced wait times.</li>
<li>Opportunities for upselling and improved order accuracy.</li>
<li>Valuable data collection for refining marketing strategies.</li>
  </ul>
</p>
<h4>Costs:</h4>
<p>
  <ul>
<li>Initial investment in hardware and software.</li>
<li>Training and integration expenses.</li>
<li>Ongoing maintenance and support requirements.</li>
  </ul>
</p>
<h3>Conclusion</h3>
<p>
This project charter serves as a foundational document guiding the implementation of tabletop menu tablets at Sauce and Spoon, emphasizing a commitment to innovation and excellence in dining experiences. The successful execution of this initiative is anticipated to yield significant business benefits and set a precedent for future technological advancements in the restaurant industry.</p>
